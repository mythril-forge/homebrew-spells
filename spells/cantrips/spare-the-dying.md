# Spare the Dying

- [x] Homebrew

***0th-level necromancy***

**Casting Time:** action

**Duration:** instantaneous

**Range:** touch

**Tags:** verbal, somatic

---

## Description
You touch a **dying** creature.
The creature regains `1d4` hit points.
This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
